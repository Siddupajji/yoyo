Our Journey

Established in 2007, we have carved a niche in precision manufacturing of metal components owing to our expertise in metal manufacturing, machining, tooling and design. After catering needs of our customers for more than 13 years, we established CTSTRENGTH INDIA LLP in 2020. We have established our self as India's only manufacturer of premium quality revolving dumbells and weight stacks. Our expertise lies in manufacturing strength equipment like Olympic plates, Olympic barbells, dumbells, weight stacks and accessories. We at CTSTRENGTH INDIA LLP strive to build robust equipment to cater every need of fitness industry.
